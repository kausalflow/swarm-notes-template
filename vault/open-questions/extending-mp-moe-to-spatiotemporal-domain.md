title: Extending MP-MoE to spatiotemporal domain
---

**Background:** The current MP-MoE framework focuses on 1D time-series modeling at the basin scale, which limits its ability to capture broader correlations across the atmospheric field.

**Question / Future Work:** Extend the MP-MoE framework from 1D time-series basin-scale modeling into the spatiotemporal domain by incorporating graph-based dependencies to ensure regional consistency in precipitation forecasts across neighboring catchments.

**Why It Matters:** Extending to the spatiotemporal domain is necessary to move beyond basin-scale accuracy and ensure physically coherent forecasts across a geographical region.

**Evidence:** Future research will focus on extending the framework to the spatiotemporal domain by incorporating graph-based dependencies, ensuring regional consistency across neighboring catchments.
