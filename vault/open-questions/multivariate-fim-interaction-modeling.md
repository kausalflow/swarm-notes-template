title: Multivariate FIM interaction modeling
---

**Background:** The influence of failure-impact modifiers (FIMs) on model quality during Federated Learning training is complex, as multiple factors like model architecture and data skew interact. This study defined scenarios based on a base configuration to isolate the effects of single modifiers.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should focus on systematically investigating and modeling the non-linear interactions and combined effects of multiple failure-impact modifiers (such as data skew, model architecture, and failure timing) to provide a more comprehensive understanding of failure propagation.

**Why It Matters:** Most real-world failures are multivariate; establishing predictive models that account for modifier interaction is essential for accurate failure prognosis.

**Evidence:** Hence, a certain combination of multiple factors is always involved. Therefore, it is not possible to exclude whether some connections between these factors are causing additional influence.
