title: Integrate richer visual inputs and VLM reasoning
---

**Background:** In generative trajectory forecasting, models trained via supervised learning often generate statistically plausible futures but may violate non-differentiable environmental rules or social norms.

**Question / Future Work:** Extend the proposed framework by incorporating richer visual inputs and Visual-Language Model (VLM)-based scene reasoning to improve general motion prediction capabilities in intelligent multimedia systems.

**Why It Matters:** Incorporating richer visual information and VLM reasoning can help ground trajectory predictions in a deeper semantic understanding of complex, dynamic scenes, moving beyond purely geometric or proximity-based scene modeling.

**Evidence:** In future work, we plan to extend the framework with richer visual inputs and VLM-based scene reasoning for more general motion prediction in intelligent multimedia systems.
