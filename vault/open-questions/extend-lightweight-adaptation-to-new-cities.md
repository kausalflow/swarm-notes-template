title: Extend adaptation to new environments
---

**Background:** The practical applicability of successful forecasting methods is often limited by the specific environmental and regulatory context in which they were developed.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is needed to assess the general applicability of lightweight adaptation strategies (like online residual correction) by extending their evaluation to heterogeneous datasets, multiple cities, and varying sensing conditions.

**Why It Matters:** Validating the framework across diverse cities and sensing conditions is necessary to confirm its practical utility beyond the single case study of Beijing.

**Evidence:** Extending lightweight adaptation strategies to heterogeneous datasets, cities, and sensing conditions will be important for assessing the broader applicability of the proposed approach.
