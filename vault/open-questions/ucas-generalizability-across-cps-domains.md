title: Generalizability across CPS domains
---

**Background:** Research in Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) safety monitoring often faces extreme class imbalance in telemetry data, which limits the effectiveness of supervised learning models.

**Question / Future Work:** Evaluate the generalizability of the proposed uncertainty-guided label rebalancing ($uLNR$) framework across additional CPS domains, particularly those with different sensor modalities and operational settings, such as autonomous submarines, to confirm its robustness beyond the tested UAV context.

**Why It Matters:** Assessing performance across diverse CPS types is crucial for establishing the broad applicability of data rebalancing techniques that rely on inferred uncertainty signals.

**Evidence:** Future work includes evaluating U-Balance across additional CPS domains, such as autonomous submarines, to assess generalisability across sensor modalities and operational settings.
