title: Online learning for gating network adaptation
---

**Background:** The MP-MoE framework uses a Gating Network that relies on the diversity of a fixed pool of pre-existing Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP) experts.

**Question / Future Work:** Explore incorporating online learning mechanisms into the Gating Network to allow it to autonomously adapt its expert selection strategy in response to seasonal climate shifts and evolving atmospheric patterns, thereby enhancing operational resilience.

**Why It Matters:** Online learning is critical for maintaining model relevance and predictive power as long-term climate statistics and weather patterns evolve.

**Evidence:** Additionally, exploring online learning mechanisms will allow the Gating Network to autonomously adapt to seasonal climate shifts and evolving atmospheric patterns, further enhancing the system’s operational resilience.
